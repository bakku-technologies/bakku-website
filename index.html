Help us out!

FES Hybrid Exoskeleton

Michael Conrad, Andrew Nagal & Mitch Gaines

Our team is actively working on this project through WPI in order to develop a fully functional exoskeleton for use by paraplegic individuals. We are doing this by using functional electrical stimulation (FES) coupled with an assistive mechanical design and an intuitive control interface.

Active Bed Sore Prevention

Mitch Gaines & Andrew Nagal

Our original prototype for this system was the grand prize winner at the MedHacks 2017 hackathon hosted by Johns Hopkins University. Our system dynamically pressure maps areas across a bed surface and provides dynamic pressure alleviation with the intent of preventing pressure ulcer formation on patients. Currently our system is being developed for wheelchair users and will be the flagship product for our company.

Robot in 3 Days

Andrew Nagal & Mitch Gaines

Following the kickoff of the 2018 FIRST Robotics Competition season, our team as well as several other colleagues participated in Robot in 3 Days (Ri3D) which challenged us to build a competitive robot for that competition in just three days time. This included two sleepless nights of mechanical, electrical, and software development in order to complete the challenge.
